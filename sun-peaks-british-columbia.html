Beyond the Slopes: Activities for Non-Skiers

Whether you're not a fan of skiing or simply want to take a break from the slopes, Sun Peaks offers a range of activities that will keep non-skiers entertained. One popular option is snowshoeing, a great way to explore the stunning winter landscapes surrounding the resort. With numerous trails to choose from, you can venture into the tranquil alpine forests, taking in the crisp mountain air while enjoying the peacefulness of nature. Guided tours are available, allowing you to learn about the local flora and fauna while immersing yourself in the beauty of the Canadian wilderness.

For those seeking a more adrenaline-filled experience, tubing provides a thrilling alternative. Grab an inflatable tube, hop on the conveyor lift, and prepare for a fast-paced ride down the groomed tubing lanes. It's a fantastic activity for individuals, families, or groups of friends looking for a fun and exhilarating time. Whether you choose to spin, slide, or race, tubing will undoubtedly leave you with smiles and lasting memories. Plus, with night tubing available, you can continue the excitement well into the evening, as the illuminated lanes create a magical atmosphere under the starry sky.
